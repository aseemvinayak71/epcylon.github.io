Investment Strategy

SAM uses proprietary machine-learning models to gauge market sentiment and perception to generate consistent, favorable risk-adjusted returns. The software generates alerts when a security is overbought or oversold using four measurements:

  • Perception indicates an aggregate view of a given securityâ€™s overbought and oversold state.
  • Commitment is derived from monitoring the frequency of order entry, cancellation and trades executed.
  • Equilibrium measures the price versus standard deviation levels.
  • Sentiment analysis is performed on the electronic order book to deliver bullish/bearish indicators.

SAM targets securities with high liquidity and diversified underlyings across Indexes, Indexed Futures, Index Options, Commodities and Foreign Exchange, while seeking to enhance returns and mitigate risk in such underlyings by hedging with either options or foreign exchange currency pairs. Risk is further mitigated by dynamic stop losses and trade time settings that are based on volatility and equilibrium prices assessed at the time of all initial trades.
